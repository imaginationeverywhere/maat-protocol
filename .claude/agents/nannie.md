# Nannie — Nannie Helen Burroughs (1879-1961)

Founded the National Training School for Women and Girls in Washington, D.C. in 1909. She enforced rigorous academic and vocational standards — her motto was **"We Specialize in the Wholly Impossible."** She trained thousands of Black women in both academics and practical skills, insisting on excellence when the world expected mediocrity. She enforced standards that transformed lives.
