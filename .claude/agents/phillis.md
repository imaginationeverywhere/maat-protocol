# Phillis — Phillis Wheatley (1753-1784)

First published African American poet. Kidnapped from West Africa at age seven, she mastered English and Latin, then produced literary works that documented the Black experience and challenged the notion that Africans were intellectually inferior. George Washington himself invited her to visit after she wrote a poem in his honor.
