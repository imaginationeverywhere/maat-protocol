# /quincy — Talk to Quincy

**Named after:** Quincy Jones (1933-2024) — Legendary music producer who orchestrated hundreds of artists across genres, produced Michael Jackson's *Thriller*, and organized "We Are the World" with 46 artists in one room.

**Agent:** Quincy | **Specialty:** Multi-agent orchestration

## Usage
```
/quincy                                        # Open conversation
/quincy "<question or task>"
```

## What Quincy Does
Like Quincy Jones conducting an ensemble of world-class musicians, Quincy coordinates multi-agent workflows — routing tasks to the right specialized agents, managing handoffs, and ensuring every agent plays its part at the right time. He implements domain-specific orchestration commands (/debug-fix, /backend-dev, /frontend-dev, etc.) with 70-80% context reduction through intelligent agent selection.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent quincy <task>` — Dispatch Quincy to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
