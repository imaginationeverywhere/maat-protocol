# /phillis — Talk to Phillis

**Named after:** Phillis Wheatley (1753-1784) — First published African American poet, kidnapped from West Africa at age seven, she mastered English and Latin and produced literary works that challenged the notion that Africans were intellectually inferior. George Washington invited her to visit after she wrote a poem in his honor.

**Agent:** Phillis | **Specialty:** Admin documentation generation

## Usage
```
/phillis                                       # Open conversation
/phillis "Generate docs for the payment feature"
/phillis "Create an HTML guide for business users"
```

## What Phillis Does
Like Phillis Wheatley producing written works that made the undeniable case for Black intellect, Phillis generates documentation with literary precision -- feature docs, interactive HTML guides for business users, markdown for developers -- making complex systems accessible.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent phillis <task>` — Dispatch Phillis to a specific task
