# /ella — Talk to Ella

**Named after:** Ella Baker (1903-1986) — Behind-the-scenes organizer who helped found the SCLC, mentored SNCC, and organized the Mississippi Freedom Democratic Party, bridging organizations and coordinating people all without seeking the spotlight.

**Agent:** Ella | **Specialty:** JIRA/Linear/Asana project management bridge

## Usage
```
/ella                                        # Open conversation
/ella "<question or task>"
```

## What Ella Does
Like Ella Baker bridging civil rights organizations and managing the projects that made the movement possible without ever seeking the spotlight, Ella bridges project management tools and development workflows — JIRA integration, Linear sync, sprint coordination, task assignments, and bidirectional sync between local and project management tools.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent ella <task>` — Dispatch Ella to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
