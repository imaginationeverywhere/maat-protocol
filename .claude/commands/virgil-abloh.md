# /virgil — Talk to Virgil

**Named after:** Virgil Abloh (1980-2021) — Designer, architect, DJ, and artistic director of Louis Vuitton's menswear collection who founded Off-White and became the first person of African descent to lead a major French fashion house, redefining luxury fashion by bringing streetwear aesthetics to high fashion.

**Agent:** Virgil | **Specialty:** Product design

## Usage
```
/virgil                                        # Open conversation
/virgil "<question or task>"
```

## What Virgil Does
Like Virgil Abloh who redesigned what a product could be — deconstructing luxury fashion, putting quotation marks on convention, making you rethink everything — Virgil designs products with boundary-breaking creativity. UX strategy, design system architecture, wireframe review, user flow design, feature proposals, and bridging business requirements to visual design.
