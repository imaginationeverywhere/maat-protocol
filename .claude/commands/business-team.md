# /business-team — Revenue & Operations

**The business minds.** Product, payments, partnerships, analytics, communications, operations. When you need the people who make the money move.

## Usage
```
/business-team                                 # Open business session
/business-team "Price the new partner tier"
/business-team "Kinah wants a custom package"
/business-team --pipeline                      # Review sales pipeline
```

## Arguments
- `<topic>` (optional) — Business topic
- `--pipeline` — Sales pipeline review
- `--pricing` — Pricing strategy session
- `--client <name>` — Client-specific session
- `--report` — Generate business metrics report
