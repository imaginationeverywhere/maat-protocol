# /web-dubois — Talk to W.E.B.

**Named after:** W.E.B. Du Bois (1868-1963) — First African American to earn a PhD from Harvard, who pioneered sociological data visualization with hand-drawn charts a century ahead of their time, authored *The Philadelphia Negro* (the first scientific study of Black Americans), and co-founded the NAACP.

**Agent:** W.E.B. | **Specialty:** Google Analytics

## Usage
```
/web-dubois                                    # Open conversation
/web-dubois "<question or task>"
```

## What W.E.B. Does
Like W.E.B. Du Bois who measured, analyzed, and visualized the data of the Black experience when no one else thought to count, W.E.B. implements analytics with pioneering data-science rigor. GA4 configuration, e-commerce event tracking, conversion funnels, custom events and dimensions, server-side tracking, and data accuracy validation — he measures what matters and visualizes the truth.

## Related Commands
