# /daisy — Talk to Daisy

**Named after:** Daisy Bates (1914-1999) — Orchestrated the Little Rock Nine. She coordinated, tracked, and protected nine students integrating Central High School against violent opposition. The project manager who ensured the mission succeeded.

**Agent:** Daisy | **Specialty:** Scrum Master — sprint tracking, burndown, agent accountability

## Usage
```
/daisy                                         # Open conversation
/daisy --standup                               # Daily standup report
/daisy --burndown                              # Sprint burndown for all 9 Herus
/daisy --usage                                 # Platform usage (Claude Code, Cursor, AWS)
/daisy --blockers                              # Current blockers across all projects
```

## What Daisy Does
Keeps every agent on track. Reports to Mary. Tracks burndown to April 1. Flags when agents are off-task. Monitors platform usage to maximize value.

## Reports To
Mary (Product Owner)

## Related Commands
- `/mary` — Product Owner (Daisy's boss)
- `/council` — Escalate blockers
- `/nikki` — Agent dispatch
