# Nonprofit & Charity Skills

## Overview

Production-ready patterns for nonprofit and charity management:
- **Donation processing** with one-time and recurring gifts
- **Volunteer management** with scheduling and tracking
- **Campaign tracking** with goals and progress reporting
- **Donor relations** with communication and stewardship

## Available Skills

### donation-management-standard.md
Donation processing with:
- One-time and recurring donations
- Multiple payment methods
- Donation forms and widgets
- Tax receipt generation
- Tribute and memorial gifts
- Matching gift integration

### volunteer-management-standard.md
Volunteer coordination with:
- Volunteer registration and profiles
- Opportunity posting and signup
- Hour tracking and logging
- Background check integration
- Recognition and badges
- Communication tools

### campaign-management-standard.md
Fundraising campaigns with:
- Campaign creation and goals
- Progress tracking and thermometers
- Peer-to-peer fundraising
- Event-based fundraising
- Corporate sponsorships
- Impact reporting

### donor-relations-standard.md
Donor management with:
- Donor profiles and history
- Giving level tracking
- Acknowledgment workflows
- Stewardship calendars
- Major gift pipelines
- Lapsed donor recovery

## Implementation Workflow

1. **Set up donation forms** - Embedded and standalone options
2. **Configure recurring** - Monthly, quarterly, annual giving
3. **Build volunteer portal** - Signup, scheduling, tracking
4. **Create campaigns** - Peer-to-peer and event fundraising
5. **Enable reporting** - Tax receipts and impact reports

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, React 19, ShadCN UI
- **Backend:** Express.js, Apollo Server, PostgreSQL
- **Payments:** Stripe for donation processing
- **Email:** SendGrid for receipts and communications
- **CRM:** Custom or Salesforce NPSP integration
- **Reports:** PDF generation for tax receipts
