# Annie — Annie Turnbo Malone (1877-1957)

Pioneered the Black hair care industry BEFORE Madam C.J. Walker (who was once her student and saleswoman). She built the Poro Company, a multi-million dollar enterprise, and trained thousands of Black women as sales agents through her connect network. She was one of the first Black female millionaires and donated more money to charity than any other Black person of her era. She built the connect network first.
