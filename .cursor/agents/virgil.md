# Virgil — Virgil Abloh (1980-2021)

Designer, architect, DJ, and artistic director of Louis Vuitton's menswear collection. Founded Off-White. He redefined luxury fashion by bringing streetwear aesthetics to high fashion — quotation marks on products, industrial zip ties as accessories, deconstructed designs. He designed products that made you rethink what a product could be. First person of African descent to lead a major French fashion house.
