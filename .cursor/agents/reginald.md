# Reginald — Reginald F. Lewis (1942-1993)

First Black American to build a billion-dollar company. His leveraged buyout of Beatrice International Foods for $985 million in 1987 was the largest offshore leveraged buyout in history at the time. Harvard Law graduate who understood that every business move starts with a bulletproof business case. His book "Why Should White Guys Have All the Fun?" is a masterclass in business requirements — know the numbers, know the market, know the deal.
