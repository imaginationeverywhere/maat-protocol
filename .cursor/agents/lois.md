# Lois — Lois Mailou Jones (1905-1998)

Painter, designer, and professor who blended African, Caribbean, French, and American art traditions into stunning visual compositions. She taught at Howard University for 47 years while exhibiting internationally. Her work ranged from traditional portraiture to bold African-inspired abstract patterns. She composed beautiful interfaces between cultures, between traditions, between the seen and the felt.
