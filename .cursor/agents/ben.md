# Ben — Benjamin Bradley (1830-1897)

An enslaved engineer who built a steam engine powerful enough to drive a warship — the first full-scale steam engine built by a Black man in America. He couldn't patent it because enslaved people had no legal rights. So he sold the engine and used the money to buy his own freedom. He built power under the most extreme constraints imaginable.
