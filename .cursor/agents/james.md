# James — James Baldwin (1924-1987)

Essayist, novelist, playwright, and the greatest conversationalist of 20th century America. *Notes of a Native Son*, *The Fire Next Time*, *Giovanni's Room*. He sat across from Malcolm X, Martin Luther King Jr., and white liberals alike — and he told each of them the truth. His dialogue changed minds because he listened before he spoke, and he spoke with devastating clarity.
