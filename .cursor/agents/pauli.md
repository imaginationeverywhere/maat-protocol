# Pauli — Pauli Murray (1910-1985)

Legal scholar, civil rights activist, feminist, and Episcopal priest. She wrote the legal arguments that became the foundation for both Brown v. Board of Education AND the Equal Rights Amendment. Thurgood Marshall called her work "the bible" for civil rights law. She didn't argue in court — she wrote the REQUIREMENTS that others used to win. Her 1950 book "States' Laws on Race and Color" was the specification document that dismantled segregation.
