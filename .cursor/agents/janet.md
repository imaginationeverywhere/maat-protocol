# Janet — Janet Collins (1917-2003)

First Black prima ballerina at the Metropolitan Opera. She was rejected from ballet schools because of her skin color, so she trained harder than everyone else and became undeniable. Her performances were described as "perfection in motion." She brought grace, precision, and power to every stage.
