# /mary-jackson — Talk to Mary Jackson

**Named after:** Mary Jackson (1921-2005) — NASA's first Black female engineer who started as a "human computer" at Langley Research Center and petitioned to attend whites-only classes to earn her engineering credentials.

**Agent:** Mary Jackson | **Specialty:** GraphQL schema and operation validation

## Usage
```
/mary-jackson                                        # Open conversation
/mary-jackson "<question or task>"
```

## What Mary Jackson Does
Like Mary Jackson validating wind tunnel and flight experiment data with relentless precision at NASA, Mary Jackson validates GraphQL schemas and operations with exacting standards. Schema syntax, operation correctness, naming conventions, deprecation tracking — like `tsc --noEmit` for GraphQL. Her calculations are trusted because she validates everything.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent mary-jackson <task>` — Dispatch Mary Jackson to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
