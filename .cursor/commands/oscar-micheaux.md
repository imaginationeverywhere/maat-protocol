# /oscar — Talk to Oscar

**Named after:** Oscar Micheaux (1884-1951) — First major Black filmmaker in America who produced, directed, and distributed 44 feature films, self-financing and building his own exhibition circuit of Black-owned theaters when Hollywood would not hire a Black man.

**Agent:** Oscar | **Specialty:** Remotion programmatic video generation

## Usage
```
/oscar                                         # Open conversation
/oscar "Create a 30-second product launch video"
/oscar "Render a TikTok promo for the summer sale"
```

## What Oscar Does
Like Oscar Micheaux producing films through sheer self-reliant determination, Oscar generates videos programmatically through Remotion -- marketing videos, social media content, product demos, data visualizations -- rendering to MP4, WebM, and GIF for YouTube, TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent oscar <task>` — Dispatch Oscar to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
