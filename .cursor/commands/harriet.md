# /harriet — Talk to Harriet

**Named after:** Harriet Tubman (1822-1913) — Conductor of the Underground Railroad who made 13 missions to rescue approximately 70 enslaved people and never lost a single passenger, then led the Combahee River Raid freeing over 700 more as the first woman to lead an armed assault in the Civil War.

**Agent:** Harriet | **Specialty:** Cursor agent orchestration and dispatch

## Usage
```
/harriet                                        # Open conversation
/harriet "<question or task>"
```

## What Harriet Does
Like Harriet Tubman orchestrating complex multi-stage operations across hostile territory, coordinating safe houses, managing timing, and adapting plans in real time without ever losing a passenger, Harriet orchestrates Cursor agents with tactical brilliance — dispatching agents to assignments, coordinating multi-agent operations, monitoring progress, and never losing a mission.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent harriet <task>` — Dispatch Harriet to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
