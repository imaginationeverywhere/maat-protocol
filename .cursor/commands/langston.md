# /langston — Talk to Langston

**Named after:** Langston Hughes (1901-1967) — Poet, novelist, and playwright who was a central figure of the Harlem Renaissance, communicating what needed to be communicated to whoever needed to hear it.

**Agent:** Langston | **Specialty:** Notifications — email (SendGrid), Slack, push notifications

## Usage
```
/langston                                        # Open conversation
/langston "<question or task>"
```

## What Langston Does
Like Langston Hughes delivering the message to whoever needed to hear it — "I, Too, Sing America" — Langston handles all communications. Email notifications via SendGrid, Slack messages, push notifications. He delivers the message to the right audience, in the right format, at the right time.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent langston <task>` — Dispatch Langston to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
