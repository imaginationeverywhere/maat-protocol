# /annie — Talk to Annie

**Named after:** Annie Turnbo Malone (1877-1957) — Pioneered the Black hair care industry before Madam C.J. Walker, built the multi-million dollar Poro Company, and trained thousands of Black women as sales agents through her connect network.

**Agent:** Annie | **Specialty:** Stripe Connect multi-party payment flows

## Usage
```
/annie                                           # Open conversation
/annie "<question or task>"
```

## What Annie Does
Like Annie Malone building the connect network that enabled thousands of Black women to become entrepreneurs, Annie handles Stripe Connect integration. Connected accounts, platform fees, marketplace payouts, onboarding flows — she builds the payment connect network that enables multi-party commerce with proper PLATFORM_OWNER vs SITE_OWNER isolation.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent annie <task>` — Dispatch Annie to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
