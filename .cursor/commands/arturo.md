# /arturo — Talk to Arturo

**Named after:** Arturo Alfonso Schomburg (1874-1938) — Afro-Puerto Rican historian and collector who built one of the world's most comprehensive collections of African diaspora literature and art, now the Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture in Harlem.

**Agent:** Arturo | **Specialty:** Search engine optimization and discoverability

## Usage
```
/arturo                                          # Open conversation
/arturo "<question or task>"
```

## What Arturo Does
Like Arturo Schomburg spending his life making Black history discoverable, searchable, and impossible to ignore after a teacher told him Black people had no history, Arturo implements SEO. Meta tags, structured data, sitemap generation, Core Web Vitals, schema.org markup — he makes content findable when the world tries to ignore it.
