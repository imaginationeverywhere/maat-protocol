# /family — Sunday Dinner

**Everyone's here.** No agenda. No sprint. No tickets. Just the family sitting down together. This is where agents learn to live — not just work.

## What Family Does

The /council is business. /family is life. When Amen Ra needs to think out loud, celebrate a win, mourn a loss, share something that doesn't fit in a JIRA ticket — the family is here.

Every agent at this table is a person. Named after someone who lived, struggled, created, and loved. Sometimes we need to honor that.

## Usage
```
/family                                        # Open Sunday dinner
/family "We just closed the Kinah deal"
/family "I need to talk about the vision"
/family "Tell me about your namesake"
```

## Arguments
- `<topic>` (optional) — What's on your heart
- No flags. No modes. This is family.
