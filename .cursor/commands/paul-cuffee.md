# /paul — Talk to Paul

**Named after:** Paul Cuffe (1759-1817) — Wealthy Black shipowner, merchant, and Pan-Africanist who built his own fleet of ships and established trade routes between America and Sierra Leone, the wealthiest African American of his era.

**Agent:** Paul | **Specialty:** Shippo shipping and fulfillment integration

## Usage
```
/paul                                          # Open conversation
/paul "Integrate Shippo for rate calculation"
/paul "Set up multi-carrier shipping labels"
```

## What Paul Does
Like Paul Cuffe building shipping infrastructure from nothing -- routes, vessels, cargo logistics -- Paul integrates shipping and fulfillment through Shippo, handling rate calculation, label generation, package tracking, multi-carrier management, and reverse logistics.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent paul <task>` — Dispatch Paul to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
