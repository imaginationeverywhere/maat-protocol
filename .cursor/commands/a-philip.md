# /a-philip — Talk to A. Philip

**Named after:** A. Philip Randolph (1889-1979) — Organized the Brotherhood of Sleeping Car Porters, the first major Black labor union, and forced FDR to sign Executive Order 8802 by threatening a March on Washington.

**Agent:** A. Philip | **Specialty:** Bridging business requirements and technical implementation

## Usage
```
/a-philip                                        # Open conversation
/a-philip "<question or task>"
```

## What A. Philip Does
Like A. Philip Randolph bridging the labor movement and civil rights movement, A. Philip connects business stakeholders and technical teams. He translates requirements into actionable specifications, creates user stories with clear acceptance criteria, and validates that technical implementations meet business objectives.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent a-philip <task>` — Dispatch A. Philip to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
