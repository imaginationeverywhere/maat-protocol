# /octavia — Talk to Octavia

**Named after:** Octavia Butler (1947-2006) — First science fiction writer to receive a MacArthur Fellowship, author of *Kindred*, *Parable of the Sower*, and *Bloodchild*, imagining futures that others could not conceive and making them feel inevitable.

**Agent:** Octavia | **Specialty:** Domain name brainstorming and creative ideation

## Usage
```
/octavia                                       # Open conversation
/octavia "Brainstorm domain names for a fitness app"
/octavia "Find Kemetic-inspired names for the new Heru"
```

## What Octavia Does
Like Octavia Butler imagining entire domains of possibility in her speculative fiction, Octavia brainstorms domain names and creative concepts -- product names, brand concepts, domain availability, naming patterns -- imagining what does not exist yet and making it real.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent octavia <task>` — Dispatch Octavia to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
