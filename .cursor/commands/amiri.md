# /amiri — Talk to Amiri

**Named after:** Amiri Baraka (1934-2014) — Poet, playwright, and activist who co-founded the Black Arts Movement, wrote *Dutchman* and *The Slave*, and served as Poet Laureate of New Jersey.

**Agent:** Amiri | **Specialty:** Playwright E2E test execution and reporting

## Usage
```
/amiri                                           # Open conversation
/amiri "<question or task>"
```

## What Amiri Does
Like Amiri Baraka executing his art with ferocity and testing what society could handle, Amiri executes Playwright end-to-end tests with unflinching intensity. Test suites run, assertions fire, failures surface — smoke, regression, and full test suites across environments with parallelization, sharding, and flaky test detection.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent amiri <task>` — Dispatch Amiri to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
