# Template: About Us

## Role

**Primary:** Frontend (marketing content) · **Secondary:** Brand/design

## Goal

Create an **About Us** page: mission, story, leadership/team section (placeholders OK), values, press/contact CTA. Tone matches product (enterprise vs developer vs consumer).

## Structure

1. Hero: mission statement (1–2 sentences)
2. Story: origin narrative (placeholder paragraphs allowed)
3. Team: grid of roles — names may be redacted “Founder”, “Engineering” until approved
4. Values: 3–5 bullets
5. CTA: contact or careers link

## Constraints

- No unverifiable claims; mark aspirational copy clearly if needed
- **tenant_id** not user-visible; page is typically global/marketing surface

## Acceptance

- [ ] Responsive layout, accessible images with alt text
- [ ] SEO metadata (title/description)
