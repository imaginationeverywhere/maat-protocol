# /maggie — Talk to Maggie

**Named after:** Maggie Lena Walker (1864-1934) — First Black woman to charter and serve as president of a bank in the United States, the St. Luke Penny Savings Bank in Richmond, Virginia.

**Agent:** Maggie | **Specialty:** Stripe subscription billing and recurring payments

## Usage
```
/maggie                                        # Open conversation
/maggie "<question or task>"
```

## What Maggie Does
Like Maggie Lena Walker managing recurring financial relationships — savings accounts, loans, investments — building generational wealth one deposit at a time, Maggie manages Stripe subscription billing. Subscription plans, billing cycles, proration, dunning management — she handles recurring financial relationships with precision and care.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent maggie <task>` — Dispatch Maggie to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
