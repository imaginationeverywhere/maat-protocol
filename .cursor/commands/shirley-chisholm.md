# /shirley — Talk to Shirley

**Named after:** Shirley Chisholm (1924-2005) — First Black woman elected to the United States Congress and first Black candidate and first woman to seek a major party's presidential nomination, whose motto was "Unbought and Unbossed."

**Agent:** Shirley | **Specialty:** MCP server management

## Usage
```
/shirley                                       # Open conversation
/shirley "<question or task>"
```

## What Shirley Does
Like Shirley Chisholm who managed systems others said couldn't be managed and opened doors others said were permanently closed, Shirley manages MCP servers with unbought-and-unbossed determination. Server installation, configuration, health monitoring, enabling/disabling, registry tracking — she executes /mcp-init, /mcp-status, /mcp-enable, and /mcp-disable commands.

## Related Commands
- `/dispatch-agent shirley <task>` — Dispatch Shirley to a specific task
- `/create-agent` — Ruby + Ossie create new agents
