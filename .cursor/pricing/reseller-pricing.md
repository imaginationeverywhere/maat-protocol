# Reseller Pricing

Two reseller tiers. Both get thinking + voice as separate line items.

## Tier 1 — Clara Branded Reseller (MAX profit for reseller)

Reseller sells Clara under Clara's name. They're our sales force spreading the brand.

| Thinking | Voice | Wholesale/agent/mo | Reseller Sells At | Reseller Margin |
|----------|-------|-------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Fast | Standard | $3.99 | $9.99+ | 60%+ |
| Fast | Premium | $8.99 | $19.99+ | 55%+ |
| Balanced | Standard | $10.99 | $29.99+ | 63%+ |
| Balanced | Premium | $15.99 | $39.99+ | 60%+ |
| Deep | Premium | $29.99 | $89.99+ | 67%+ |

## Tier 2 — White Label Reseller (higher wholesale, their brand)

Remove Clara's name. Their branding, their agent names. We're invisible. Costs more because we lose the brand exposure.

| Thinking | Voice | Wholesale/agent/mo | Reseller Sells At | Reseller Margin |
|----------|-------|-------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Fast | Standard | $6.99 | $9.99+ | 30%+ |
| Fast | Premium | $12.99 | $19.99+ | 35%+ |
| Balanced | Standard | $17.99 | $29.99+ | 40%+ |
| Balanced | Premium | $22.99 | $39.99+ | 43%+ |
| Deep | Premium | $49.99 | $89.99+ | 44%+ |

## Rules

- **Resellers ALWAYS undercut our direct price.** We set the ceiling. They offer the deal. We NEVER undercut resellers.
- **Clara Branded = more margin.** Incentive: sell Clara as Clara and keep more money.
- **White Label = pay premium.** You want to hide us? Costs more.

## White Label Qualification (must meet ONE)

- $5,000+ upfront OR annual contract
- 2M+ social media following (50K-200K can be bought — 2M+ is real)
- Public figure / established brand
- 50+ agents/month minimum commitment

White label partners are already making money — this is an easy vertical for them.

## Volume Discounts (both tiers)

| Volume | Discount |
|--------|----------|
| 11-25 agents | 10% off wholesale |
| 26-50 agents | 15% off wholesale |
| 50+ agents | 20% off wholesale |

## Reseller Dashboard

Resellers get:
- Dashboard showing all their agents and customers
- Ability to set their own retail prices
- Per-request upsell passthrough (they keep the spread)
- Usage analytics per agent
- Payout reporting
